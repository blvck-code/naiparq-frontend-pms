About Us

We are the convenient parking solution you can trust. Established in 2021, our main goal has been to solve the parking problem in cities. The surge in parking demand has led to mounting pressure to expand the supply of parking infrastructure through the conversion of unused land into private parking spaces, and
the construction of multi-level parking facilities within buildings.
A parking facility has the potential to generate revenue for its proprietor, while simultaneously providing motorists with the advantages of convenience, Pay-as-you-Go pricing, and security. We address the parking predicament by catering to the requirements of both ends of the spectrum, namely parking facility owners and motorists.

How we solve your Parking Problems

We currently have two solutions:

We are the airbnb of parking for parking – where motorists can reserve a parking space in advance. We look at a parking space as a form of real estate that can be ‘rented’ and can generate revenue for the owner. This solution works to open up underutilized spaces in the city by empowering everyone and anyone with idle parking space or even idle land to list and advertise their spaces on the Naiparq platform. The process is simple, a parking space owner would list his/her spaces on the Naiparq platform while a motorist would download the Naiparq mobile app on their phone and use it to find and reserve this space.

Our parking management solution incorporates access control features and employs License Plate Recognition (LPR) software to automate the parking process, augment facility security, and minimize the risk of unauthorized access. This comprehensive system is capable of managing both commercial parking lots, which require payment for parking, as well as non-commercial parking lots, such as those found at office blocks or residential areas, where parking is free of charge. By implementing our parking management system, owners and managers can efficiently manage their parking facilities while providing a seamless and secure experience for their customers or tenants.
